# Recipe: 20-Minute Garlic Chicken Skillet

Pat 2 boneless chicken breasts dry and season with salt and black pepper. Heat 2 tablespoons of
olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat, then sear the chicken 5-6 minutes per side until
golden and cooked through. Lower the heat, add 4 minced garlic cloves and a squeeze of fresh lemon,
and spoon the garlicky pan juices over the chicken. Finish with chopped parsley and serve.
